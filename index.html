Our Vision for Online Learning

Guiding students towards authentic Islamic knowledge with balance and moderation.

A Beacon of Knowledge

We aspire for the virtual institute to be a beacon of knowledge, combining authenticity with modernity, and providing a comprehensive educational environment for the students, wherever they may be, to learn the principles of their religion and its culture with a balanced and moderate approach—neither extreme nor negligent.

Continuing the Mission

We aim for the institute to continue the mission of Imam Hassan Cisse (may Allah have mercy on him) in spreading beneficial knowledge, promoting the correct understanding of Islam, and connecting new generations with the language of their Religion, helping them live with awareness and confidence in their societies.

Our Goals

Dedicated objectives for our online Arabic Language and Islamic Studies courses.

Easy Knowledge Access

Learn Islamic creeds and Arabic fundamentals comfortably from home via well-designed online programs, saving time and effort.

Correct Islamic Creed

Instill authentic creed based on Ahl al-Sunnah wal-Jama‘ah, avoiding extremism, with a clear, accessible approach.

Arabic Language Mastery

Learn and master Arabic to comprehend the Quran and Sunnah and fulfill daily needs.

Balanced & Moderate Method

Adhere to moderation—neither excess nor deficiency—ensuring a well-rounded understanding aligned with Islam's essence.

Continuing Imam Hassan Cisse's Legacy

Continue the educational approach of Imam Hassan Cisse (RA) propagated through AAII since 1988.

Serving Global Students

Focus on serving Muslim communities in America, Europe, and worldwide with practical, accessible learning.

Utilizing Modern Technology

Use the latest interactive online tools, allowing flexible learning according to student schedules.

Effective Knowledge Transfer

These online courses aim to spread authentic knowledge delivered by well rounded tutors and connect Muslims in the West with their Islamic culture and identity.
